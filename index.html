Twitch Plays Tamagotchi

Here's a new challenge for Twitch: take care of a living thing (Tamagotchis are real, OK). Twitch became a Pok√©mon champion numerous time, it recently installed Linux, but can it make a Tamagotchi live? Each 5 seconds the most popular button in the Twitch chat will be sent to a Tamagotchi.

How long will it live? That's for you to know, and decide.

How does it work?

What? There are Tamagotchi emulators? Nope, we use a real Tamagotchi (1997, made of good old translucent plastic). Viewer's input are relayed to the Tamagotchi by a robot finger. Appreciate the joy of telerobotic!
